How stores break

Most Shopify stores don't have a
traffic problem.

01 /
🐌

Performance Drag

Symptom → Root Cause

Stacked third-party apps, render-blocking scripts, unoptimised images. Each adds 200–400ms. Compounded, they cut your conversion rate in half before a customer reads your headline.

02 /
🕳️

Conversion Leaks

Symptom → Root Cause

Confusing product hierarchies, missing social proof at decision points, weak variant UX, no sticky conversion bars. Customers arrive and leave without ever reaching "Add to Cart."

03 /
🏗️

Architecture Debt

Symptom → Root Cause

Theme customizations layered on top of customizations. Metafields inconsistent or missing. No scalable product data model. Marketing can't move fast because engineering is always firefighting.
